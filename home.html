Welcome to the HS MSA Council

Mission

We are soon to be a 501(c) high school student-run, non-profit organization that oversees the individual MSAs in the greater NYC area and strives to facilitate networking, educating, and empowering high school Muslim students. We support individual MSAs and promote brotherhood and sisterhood, and the wellbeing of students, including their spiritual, religious, social, and civic growth.
